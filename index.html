Express interest

Schools, troupes, language programs, veterans, pageants, merchants, and Vietnamese-led partners — each group as its own parade unit, marching in sequence with peer Vietnamese / Little Saigon units. Costume OK; traditional optional.

Email us with interest, rough headcount, and what you might bring (signs, instruments, lion, áo dài). We’ll follow up on registration, waivers, and day-of details once you’re in.

Email krewe@cpalss.com

Golden Harvest registration

Parade entries register with Golden Harvest — not through this site. We help confirmed krewe groups line up the paperwork.

  • Online registration (or download the form) on the Golden Harvest site.
  • Group leaders sign a participant waiver. If a leader is under 18, a parent or guardian also signs the minor waiver. Minor marchers need a parent/guardian signature on the participant form. (Same idea as the 2025 packet — expect similar for 2026.)
  • Marching groups: Golden Harvest Parade asks for between 15 and 300 people per entry. If you don’t have 15, let us know — and don’t register yet — we’ll band smaller groups together into a single entry. If your group is too large, split it in half and register each group separately.

FAQ

Is there a dress code?
Not currently — no required uniform. The parade is televised, though, so dress in a way that shows your club or group identity (colors, shirts, banners, traditional wear if you like). Costume OK; traditional optional.
